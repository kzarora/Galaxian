Dice



There are three dices one of the die is biased by the probabilities P(1),P(2),P(3),P(4),P(5),P(6). And other two dices are regular dices. If all three dices tossed together then what is the probabilities of getting a sum S.

INPUT:
T is the number of test cases.
Followed by two lines for a test case first line contains Six space seperated numbers denotiong occurence of each face while testing biasedness of Dice.
And second line containing S(sum on all three dices).
OUTPUT:
Contains the proabilities of corresponding test case.

Example:
input:
1
3 3 2 3 1 3
7

Output:
0.074074
