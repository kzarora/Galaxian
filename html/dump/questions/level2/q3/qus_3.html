Zig Zag Arrangement


Miss Rosy is very keen about the classroom activities, recently in the class she assigned a new task to the the students to make a queue in order such that the person who is standing adjacent to him must be either taller or shorter but not both. Find the arrangement with minimum number of transitions(swapings) required for this activity given student at 1st possition is taller to 2nd one .

Input:
The first line of input contains the number of testcases t.
The next line contains no. of students(n).
The next line contains height of n students.

Output:
For each testcase print the heights of the students in the required format.

Constraints:
1<=t<=100000
1<=n<=3000
0<=a[i]<=100000

Example:
1
7
1 3 2 5 1 8 9

Output
3 1 5 1 8 2 9
