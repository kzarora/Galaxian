Confused Boy


Anuranjan was facing any direction and starts walking to reach his destination,In between he turn Right or Left after some distances.After travelling some distances he forget his direction.
Since Anuranjan doesn't have any compas with him. He got confused, he need your help. Are you able to find his direction.

Input:
A is the number of test cases
Each test case consists a single line of input as a string.

Output:
Final direction of Anurajan

Example:
Input
2
E10L30R20L10L15
N20R12R11R100L130

Output
West
South
